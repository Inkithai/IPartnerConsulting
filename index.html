About Us

About Our Company

iPartner Consulting launched in 2014 to bridge healthcare and technology, supporting IT clients and hospitals with EHR implementations across North America. During COVID-19, we scaled rapidly to address critical healthcare staffing needs, serving hospitals, clinics, LTC facilities, and senior homes. Today, our staffing division delivers top talent in both healthcare and technology, while our consulting division offers innovative services like Interview-as-a-Service, custom application development, software implementation, and workforce solutions—driving efficiency and success for our clients.

Staffing

Healthcare Staffing

Founded in 2014, iPartner Healthcare is a MWBE-certified staffing firm and one of the fastest-growing companies in the U.S. We specialize in healthcare staffing solutions, driven by a relentless commitment to client satisfaction. Our dedicated recruitment team ensures smooth placements, with 24/7 customer support to address your needs anytime. We view our clients as our greatest asset, personally interviewing and rigorously screening each candidate to guarantee a perfect match for your organization’s specific requirements.

We deliver skilled healthcare professionals, including RNs, LPNs, and Allied health professionals, across the nation, providing support to help organizations attract, retain, and nurture top talent in the industry.
